Where the old “$510 pool” came from — and why it was wrong for you

That figure assumed ~$85/drive on US manufacturer-recert channels (e.g. ServerPartDeals-class stock). That is not an EU shelf price, and importing those drives fights your constraint. Your find — €180 each inside Europe — is the planning number below: 6 × €180 = €1,080 for the pool alone.

Why the bill feels large (EU edition)

At €180/drive the disks are ~two-thirds of an ECC Mid all-in build. The host is still only a few hundred euros; ECC Mid vs Non-ECC Mid is mostly the DIMMs (~€30–40), not a second platform.

Disk plan: 6× 4 TB Seagate Exos (recert, EU)

Enterprise Exos SATA CMR remains a valid Cart S choice (real CMR, high workload rating). Recert in Europe is simply much closer to new-drive money than US fire-sale listings. RAIDZ2 on six identical 4 TB drives → roughly ~14 TB usable.

ECC Mid vs Non-ECC Mid

Same board, same CPU, same case — swap in ECC UDIMMs. Premium is roughly €30–40 on the host (tiny next to €1,080 of disks). After install, confirm ECC is active (TrueNAS UI / edac-util / dmidecode).

Other configurations

N100 6-SATA mini

Low idle watts; host roughly ~€450. With your Exos pool + UPS ≈ ~€1,660. Confirm SATA count and whether that SKU actually supports ECC before paying for it.

Phased buy

Buy ECC Mid host + UPS now (~€670). Buy all six identical Exos when you are ready — still plan on ~€1,080 unless an EU seller undercuts €180. Do not mix capacities in one RAIDZ2 vdev.

Used Xeon path

Cheapest true ECC on the EU second-hand market; expect fan swaps and verify ECC UDIMM (not RDIMM) for E3 boards. Prefer EU sellers to avoid import/VAT surprises.

Shopping checklist

  1. Six identical Exos (or other CMR) from an EU seller — datasheet CMR, SATA.
  2. Onboard SATA ≥6 or HBA flashed to IT mode (never hardware RAID).
  3. Boot SSD separate from the pool.
  4. Gold PSU with HDD spin-up margin; UPS.
  5. If ECC: CPU and board both list ECC UDIMM; verify after boot.
  6. Park the Crosshair for compute; do not trap it under the live pool.

Host lines are EU street estimates (Idealo / Alternate / local class), not quotes. Disk line is your stated floor: €180 × 6. Re-check before buying — HDD prices move.
