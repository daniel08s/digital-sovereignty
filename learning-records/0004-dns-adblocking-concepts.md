# DNS ad-blocking & encrypted upstream concepts understood

The learner confirmed understanding of Lesson 3: network-wide blocking via a DNS
sinkhole (AdGuard Home), why one DHCP-assigned resolver covers every device, and
encrypted upstream (DoH/DoT to a public resolver such as Quad9) so the ISP cannot
read lookups.

Why it matters: the third mission goal (block ads/trackers for the whole house)
has a solid conceptual floor. Teaching can leave the DNS/ad-blocking track and
move to storage & data reliability — the remaining mission pillar before the
media stack. Hands-on AdGuard build details can be revisited on request; do not
re-teach the sinkhole / upstream model.
