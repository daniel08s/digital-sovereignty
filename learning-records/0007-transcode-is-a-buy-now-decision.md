# Hardware transcode is a buy-now host decision

The learner correctly challenged parking Jellyfin GPU/transcode until the
media-stack lesson: if the CPU/board is purchased without a usable iGPU/dGPU,
you cannot retrofit that cheaply. What can wait is Jellyfin dashboard settings
and Docker device passthrough — not the silicon.

Why it matters: Lesson 7 now treats “media on this box?” as a sixth host
checklist item (direct play vs transcode; Intel iGPU default when shared).
Future shopping advice must not re-park the GPU fork.
